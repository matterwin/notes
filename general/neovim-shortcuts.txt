# Nvim setups

cd ~/.config/nvim/lua/custom

chadrc.lua
init.lua


NvChad: 

Cheat Sheet:									space + c + h

Colors:
Catppuccin
Doomchad 



Find files across the directory:						space + f + f

Close current buffer:								space + x

Find files in opened buffers:							space + f + b

Mark files visually:								m




New Tab:									:tabnew

Vertically split buffer:								:vsp or cntrl + w + v

Horizontally split buffer:							:sp or cntrl + w + s

Open new empty buffer:							cntrl + w + n

Open terminal:								space + h

Close terminal: (must be in terminal mode)					cntrl + d

Move around buffers/terminals:						cntrl + h || j || k || l

Close window/buffer:								cntrl + w + q
